Overview

Birthright is a competitive 1v1 strategy card game where players manage a growing gold economy and deploy units to bring an opponent's health from 25 to 0. Turns move through drawing, resource management, and attack/defense phases, with combat resolved through careful unit placement rather than chance. Built with Godot and C#, with a custom Rust server module running on SpacetimeDB for real-time multiplayer networking.
