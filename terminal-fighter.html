Terminal Fighter

Terminal Fighter is an independently developed head-to-head fighter with responsive movement, simple combat, ability-driven interactions, and a minimalist arena focused on spacing and timing. It features two unique characters, each with their own combat identity and kit (basic attack, two abilities, and passive traits). Watch the videos below to see more!
