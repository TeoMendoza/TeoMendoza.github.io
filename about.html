Who I Am

Hi, I'm Teo, born and raised in Berkeley, California, where beach days were common and sunscreen was usually an afterthought. I take snacks seriously: the good ones live in plain sight—technically not hidden, easy to reach, somehow hard to see. I like long, easy conversations and small adjustments that make things better one step at a time.

When I'm not at my desk working, I'm usually out on a late night walk with friends, especially in summer, when the cold night time breeze turns stories into permanent inside jokes. I care a lot about balance, knowing when to let loose and when to focus is something that I try to get a little bit better at every day.

My Interests

Running & Lifting: Time away from work that steadies my mood. Even better with a friend, you can have a good conversation and some laughs, and it still feels productive.

Cooking & Food Photos: My Notes app probably has more recipes than actual notes, although I mostly cook by feel at this point. Half the fun is plating (which somehow takes longer than cooking) and snapping a photo.

Favorite Eats: I love cooking, but eating wins. A ribeye with a proper sear, tacos of almost any kind, or a classic smash burger with fries, can't beat it.

Cliff Jumping: Oregon spoiled me with jump spots. That moment off the edge, especially if you do a flip, is pure adrenaline.

Hiking & Outdoors: Long trails, amazing views, and time with people I care about while getting some miles in? Count me in.
