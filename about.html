About Me

Who I Am

I'm Teo — a curious, pretty energetic person who loves building things and then refining them until they feel right. I grew up in Berkeley, California, where weekends were a mix of family time, long walks, and small creative detours. I like thoughtful conversations, making tiny improvements, and the feeling of chipping away at a hard problem until it clicks.

When I'm not at my desk, I'm usually moving — running, lifting, or exploring a new route — and then cooking something simple (often healthier than it looks). I'm big on music during long walks, small routines, and noticing the little details that make spaces feel alive.

My Interests

Running & Lifting: slow, steady progress — I like tracking, tinkering, and feeling stronger over time.

Cooking Experiments: high-protein desserts, air-fryer science, and better-than-it-should-be breakfasts.

Games as Tiny Worlds: how rules, art, and sound shape meaning — especially when small choices change the vibe.

Walks & Music: head-clearing playlists and long loops around the neighborhood.

Travel Moments: new places, cheap coffee, and photos that inspire new 3D spaces.
