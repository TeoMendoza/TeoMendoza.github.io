3D Brawler

3D Brawler is a fast-paced, skill-based free-for-all brawler with grounded physics and quick respawns. The loop is intentionally non-punishing so players can take risks, push aggressive plays, and keep the action chaotic and fun. Distinct character identities and clean, readable combat reward timing, positioning, and adaptation.
