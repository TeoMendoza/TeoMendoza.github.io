Pedalogical

Pedalogical is an educational platform that leverages AI to support both students and instructors. It provides guided learning experiences, personalized feedback, and tools for instructors to analyze student progress and adapt their teaching.

Visit Website

My Role in Pedalogical

I served as project manager during summer development, leading a three-person student team on this NSF-funded research initiative. My focus was on aligning technical work with educational goals and fostering collaboration across developers, researchers, and faculty.

In this role, some of my main contributions included:

  • Directing production, setting milestones, and guiding team workflows
  • Acting as liaison between the development team and faculty project lead
  • Overseeing system redesign efforts to ensure scalability for classroom deployment
  • Encouraging cross disciplinary communication and collaboration
  • Preparing the project for launch in Fall 2025, supporting 100-150 students

This role gave me valuable experience in leadership, project organization, and translating big-picture vision into actionable progress across a complex team project.
