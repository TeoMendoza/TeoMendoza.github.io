Pedalogical

Pedalogical is an NSF-funded educational platform that leverages AI to support both students and instructors. It provides guided learning experiences, personalized feedback, and tools for instructors to analyze student progress and adapt their teaching.

Visit Website

What Pedalogical Offers

For Instructors:

  • Analytics dashboards showing student struggles and progress
  • AI-generated summaries of student engagement and misconceptions
  • Customizable teaching modules and planning tools

For Students:

  • Interactive modules designed to reinforce understanding
  • Personalized AI-guided feedback and review questions
  • Supplemental material and practice tools to further growth

My Role in Pedalogical

As project manager, I oversaw production during summer development. My responsibilities included:

  • Coordinating a multi-person team of student developers and researchers
  • Managing project direction, milestones, and workflow
  • Leading the design and implementation of core platform features
  • Shaping the pedagogical study design and evaluation strategy

This role gave me extensive experience in team leadership, cross-disciplinary collaboration, and large-scale project execution.
