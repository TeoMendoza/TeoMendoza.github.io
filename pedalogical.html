Pedalogical

Pedalogical is an educational platform, built collaboratively, that leverages AI to support both students and instructors. It provides guided learning experiences, personalized feedback, and tools for instructors to analyze student progress and adapt their teaching.

Visit Website

My Role in Pedalogical

I served as project manager during summer development, leading a three person student team on this NSF-funded research initiative. My focus was on aligning technical work with educational goals and fostering collaboration among developers, researchers, and faculty. My key contributions included:

  • Directing production, setting milestones, and guiding team workflows
  • Acting as liaison between the development team and the faculty project lead
  • Overseeing system redesigns to ensure scalability for classroom deployment
  • Encouraging cross-disciplinary communication and collaboration
  • Preparing the platform for Spring 2025 launch, supporting ~150 students

This role gave me valuable experience in leadership, project organization, and translating big picture vision into actionable progress across a complex team project.
