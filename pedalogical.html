Pedalogical

Pedalogical is an educational platform that leverages AI to support both students and instructors. It provides guided learning experiences, personalized feedback, and tools for instructors to analyze student progress and adapt their teaching.

Visit Website

My Role in Pedalogical

As project manager, I led the summer development of Pedalogical and coordinated a three person student team as part of an NSF-funded research initiative. I worked closely with our faculty lead to translate educational research goals into concrete design and development tasks, ensuring consistent progress across technical and pedagogical fronts.

  • Directed production and workflow, setting milestones and guiding implementation cycles
  • Acted as liaison between faculty lead and the development team, aligning goals and execution
  • Oversaw the redesign of core features to support scalability and classroom deployment
  • Guided development of analytics and insight systems to track student learning patterns
  • Contributed to UX improvements including a new navigation flow and cleaner UI design
  • Collaborated on infrastructure improvements, preparing the project for launch in Fall 2025 to support 100-150 students across multiple disciplines

This experience gave me hands-on leadership in managing a cross-functional project, balancing research requirements with design, technical development, and long-term vision.
