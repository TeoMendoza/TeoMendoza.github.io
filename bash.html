Overview

Bash is a fast-paced free-for-all shooter centered on pure combat, quick respawns, and constant player interaction. The core design goal was to turn the classic lobby mini-game into the full experience, stripping away downtime and focusing entirely on mechanical skill, positioning, pressure, and adaptation. Every major system, from combat flow to character design, supports a tight gameplay loop built for nonstop action and expressive play.
